Pet Biography

Fiona and I have been rasing backyard chickens since she took an agricultural class in highschool. Our city allowed 3 backyard chickens, so we built a coop and have had chickens ever since.

They live happy lives in our yard, and give us about an egg a day during the warm seasons. Thanks ladies!

Names: Roadie, Cluck Cluck, Chikpea, Belle, Peaches, & Pepper
Breed: Chickens!
Age: Youngest is 3, Oldest is 6
Gender: All Female
Color: Varied
