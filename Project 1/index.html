Pets of Kevin Sloan

Number of Pets in the Family

I have a total 8 pets in the home as of this year (2023).

  • 1 Dog
  • 1 Cat
  • 3 Ferrets
  • 3 Chickens

Pet Photos & Bio

Mochi the Dog

Mochi is a 3 year old male Shiba Inu, and we have had him since he was a tiny puppy.
He's very anti-social and doesn't like big events or going out in public, but is big on cuddling with family.
One of his favorite passtimes is to play ball in the yard, and he is very good at catching it after it bounces one time.
Side note: He's 100% a mommas boy.

Trip the Cat

Trip is a family rescue that we took in after my wife's grandma had passed away and he needed a home.
He has always been a spoiled only child, so he has had some difficulties adjusting to having a bigger dog brother.
He'll get over it!

Natsu, Miku, and Yuki the Ferrets


Natsu is our only male ferret, and consequently our biggest.
He loves to play by rolling around colorful plastic balls with bells inside. They're actually cat toys, but he loves it.



Miku is Natu's sister and likes to play by hunting her toys, and carrying them to a hiding spot.
She especially loves tease toys that are on the end of a string, and will go crazy for a squeaker toy.


Yuki is a rescue that found a home in us thanks to a close friend.
She was actually found outside in the middle of winter behind a restruant. She was so cold she ran up to a friend of ours and tried to go up their pantleg.
Fun fact: we named her Yuki which means Snow and she doesn't grow hair on the tip of her tail. We think that may have been because of frostbite.
Don't worry though, she's been living a healthy warm life with us for 3 years now. :)

Peaches, Chickpea, and Pepper: our Chickens


My Wife and I have had backyard chickens for about 6 years now.
It is a small flock of 3, as that is the legal limit in the residental area where we live.
All of our chickens are girls, and we get plenty of eggs in the summer.

I hope you enjoyed this small student project/tour of our furry and feathery family. Have a great day!
